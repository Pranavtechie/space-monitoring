## What is this? 

"Make information very easy to comprehend and traverse, at any level of abstraction"

- List all consortiums, organizations, challenges, and clubs/ universities and their respective projects, programmes. This is a holisitic view of the people who will be the next generation of workforce in the space industry. From middle-school, to universities, to cutting-edge research labs.
- Make it easier to find people, organizations based on your interests (propulstion, computers, etc.)
- Make a comprehensive resource collection. (MIT OpenCourseware, YouTube playlists, books, etc.)
- An inter-connected knowledge graph that can be explored. (My core never-ending idea).
